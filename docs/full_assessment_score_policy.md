# Full Assessment score policy

NICO Full Assessment scores are evidence-bound.

A score may change only when attached evidence changes or when an explicit scoring defect is repaired. Missing evidence does not count as passing evidence. Scanner execution does not establish a clean finding result. Trust and export gates may reduce claims or require review, but they do not raise technical scores. Client delivery remains blocked until a human reviewer approves the same-run final report. Weight or threshold changes require a separate pull request, regression tests, and a written reason; they must not be used to recover a preferred score.
